Entry #1 - A PC Case


Introduction

I enjoy high strength feeling things. A lot of current PC cases are made of either thin materials and/or do not fit the hardware that I want to run.

For quite some time, I have been contemplating and wanting to design and build my own PC Case. I initially started out with a sheet of 1.2mm 304 SS and a 3mm plate of grade 5 titanium. BIG waste!!! I couldn't cut it with the tools that I had. - This was a few years ago.


I recently stumbled accross Gunpla Gundam hobby model building, painting and customisation videos on YouTube. These sparked my interest to do something similar. I figured that re-starting my PC Case building mission may do so.

Being inspired by the Gunpla videos, I needed to design something that had a lot of shapes and edges to allow for weathering and uniqueness. Also, being me. I had to have something made out of a unique material selection - yet still workable with a limited amount of tools. I am yet to aquire anything fancy


My tool set consisted of:

  • 1x Jigsaw
  • 1x Hacksaw
  • 1x File

...Therefore, I chose Aluminium...
